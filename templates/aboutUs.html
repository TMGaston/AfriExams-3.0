About AfriExams

AfriExams is a startup company made up of dynamic young persons with aim in impacting the society with theeir revolutionary concept, in the education on Cameroon and beyond. It is based on Information and Communication Technologie, with as outcome a mobile app, and a web app used in facilitating examinations of scholars and students. It was founded in 2016 by Mr. Bonteh Engelbert and latter on cofounded by Mr Tchinda Moise G who put together their diverse talents and ideas to reach the world.
